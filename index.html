Family Secret Fried Catfish

About

This recipe is an old family secret, you better save it for yourself before I have to take it down or risk being kicked out of my family for giving all our secrets away! this recipe was created by my great-grandmother, Hattie Stevens, and grandmother Patsy Riggs. Those two women deserve all the credit for this amazing fried catfish recipe. So you'd better thank my Nannie and Grannie for this delicious family recipe ya'll!

Ingredients

  • Catfish
  • Cornmeal
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Red Pepper

Directions

  1. Soak Fish in enough milk to cover them. let them soak for at least 30 minues.
  2. Take them out of the milk and put them on a rack or paper towels. Let fish sit for up to 5 minutes.
  3. Dredge fish in mixture of cornmeal, salt, peper and red pepper if you want it to have a little spice
  4. Put fish in hot grease and fry to a golden brown.
  5. Remove from grease, allow to cool just a bit and enjoy!
